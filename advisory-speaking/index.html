Advisory & Speaking

Chuck Herrin is CEO and Managing Principal of Herrin Advisory, where he provides strategic advisory, board briefings, and keynote speaking on cybersecurity, AI, geopolitical risk, and technology leadership. He writes the Global Race Condition intelligence newsletter, synthesizing signals across geopolitics, cybersecurity, AI, quantum computing, and macroeconomics for business and technology leaders. Herrin has over two decades of experience protecting some of the world's largest enterprises as a CISO, CTO, and board member across financial services, insurance, and technology, including roles at F5, AIG, Athene, and Texas Capital Bank. He has delivered 60+ international keynotes, published 45+ articles as a Forbes Technology Council member, and holds CISSP, CCSP, and NACD.DC board director certifications.
